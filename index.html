Finding web solutions for over 25 years

Since the earliest days of the web, Niki has been programming - increasing her portfolio of scripting languages, as technologies improved. She is also a project manager and application & database designer.

Based in Shepperton (NW Surrey), Niki's background was originally marketing but learned Classic ASP/SQL to program an Intranet to solve communication problems between development, sales and marketing. At the time, she was Director of International Marketing at a software house.

This marketing background has held her in good stead since she started consulting in 2001, as she is able to give start-ups and SME clients a complete solution, including developing a marketing plan, website, social media strategy, advertising, branding, marketing assistance, together with designing and programming bespoke CRMs with pipeline, lead and opportunity tracking and reporting. She hosts and maintains 99% of the websites she creates and is a Nominet registrar (Tag: NMCONSULTING)

Niki has produced web applications as varied as 'just-in-time' production solutions, CRM and lead reporting, messaging, diaries, event organisation, inventory, data analysis, warehousing, accounts, Point of Sale (POS) systems, sales reporting, news, membership, BACS payments and utilising/linking APIs (connections to other databases and programs). All sites are optimised and multi-platform tested, using a mobile-first approach. Anti-SQL injection techniques are used and passwords are cryptographically hashed by default.

Clients have ranged from politicians to pubs and motor racing to manufacturing.

On the personal side, Niki is an avid traveller, petrolhead and amateur astrologer.

She promotes the excellent work of WaterAid and speaks at schools to encourage girls to study STEM subjects. Niki was the National Chairman (then President) of a women's organisation and is Treasurer of her local volunteer Business Community, which organises Shepperton Big Tree Night.

She set up the successful Boat CO Safe campaign to get the law changed to require all boats in UK waterways to be fitted with a Carbon Monoxide alarm.

Niki was awarded an MBE in the 2017 New Year's Honours List and was a Professional Member of the British Computing Society (BCS).
